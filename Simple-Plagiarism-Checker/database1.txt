Plagiarism refers to the practice of using someone else's work or ideas without proper acknowledgment. It is considered a serious academic and ethical offense. With the rise of digital content, detecting plagiarism has become increasingly important in educational and professional settings. Many institutions use plagiarism detection tools to ensure originality and maintain integrity. 

Preventing plagiarism involves understanding how to properly cite sources and developing strong research and writing skills. Encouraging creativity, originality, and honesty helps build a culture of academic excellence. Technology now plays a major role in identifying copied content using various similarity and pattern-matching techniques.
